American Wildlife

LThe fauna of the United States of America includes species of mammals,reptiles,species of insects and birds. Continental United States and its surrounding islands and seas has most distinctive indigenous wildlife, including the gray wolf, American Black bear,White Tailed Deer and threatened lynx. Other known wild animals of America also includes North American river otter,American mink, Ocelot,Red Fox,American beaver,American badger and polar bear.
